Hello

I'm Jelaine

I'm a BS Information Technology graduate from Bulacan State University, specializing in Web and Mobile Applications Development. I graduated Summa Cum Laude.

I wear multiple hats: I design intuitive interfaces in Figma, build them with React, Next.js, Vue, and Nuxt, and ensure quality through structured QA testing and detailed documentation.

Beyond code, I handle graphic design and social media management. I enjoy the full creative cycle from concept to polished output.

Based in Malolos, Bulacan. Open to remote and on-site opportunities.
