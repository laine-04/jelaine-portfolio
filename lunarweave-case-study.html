Overview

The Brief

LunarWeave is a personal concept project — a fictional fashion e-commerce brand built around the idea of quiet luxury: high-end clothing that is refined and elevated, yet wearable every day. The goal was to design a full website experience that communicates the brand's identity before a visitor even reads a word.

This project was entirely self-directed, from defining the brand concept to delivering the final Figma prototype.

Process

How I Approached It

01

Moodboarding

I started by gathering visual references that captured the quiet luxury aesthetic — neutral palettes, editorial photography, and clean whitespace. This helped define the visual language before touching Figma.

02

Color Palette and Typography

I chose a muted, warm color palette and paired serif and sans-serif typefaces to reinforce the brand's elegance. Every font and color choice was intentional — understated but distinct.

03

Layout and Design

With branding locked in, I moved into Figma to build the full page layout — hero, product grid, brand story section, featured collection, and more.

Key Decisions

Design Choices

Visual-First Layout

Models and clothing take center stage. Text is kept minimal — the products and photography do the talking, which aligns with how luxury brands typically present themselves.

Quiet Color Palette

No loud colors. The palette uses deep teals, off-whites, and dark neutrals to feel premium without being flashy — matching the "quiet luxury" concept.

Editorial Sections

Sections are spaced generously and structured like editorial spreads rather than typical e-commerce grids, giving each product moment room to breathe.

Minimal Text Copy

Short, purposeful headlines only. The design leans on imagery and whitespace rather than lengthy descriptions, keeping the experience clean and immersive.

Outcome

Personal project. Designed and prototyped entirely in Figma as a self-directed exploration of quiet luxury fashion branding and e-commerce UX.
