Overview

The Brief

LunarWeave is a personal project, a fictional fashion e-commerce brand built around the concept of quiet luxury: high end clothing that is still wearable everyday. I designed the whole website experience myself, starting from the brand concept up to the final Figma prototype.

I handled everything on this project, from the branding to the layout to the final design.

Process

How I Approached It

01

Moodboarding

I collected the visual references I need and looked at existing clothing and fashion websites to know what a fashion website actually contains. I decided on the aesthetics and the branding of it based on the concept of the fictional brand I am creating a website for.

02

Color Palette and Typography

I picked a muted, warm color palette and paired a serif font with a sans-serif font to match the brand's elegance.

03

Layout and Design

Once the branding was set, I moved into Figma and built out the full page layout, from the hero section, product grid, brand story section, and featured collection.

Key Decisions

Design Choices

Visual First Layout

For a fashion brand, the clothing and the models lead the page. Keeping in mind the brand concept and how luxury brands present themselves, the text is kept minimal to let the photography showcase the products.

Color Palette

I used deep teals, off whites, and dark neutrals. No bright colors, since this fits the quiet luxury concept better than a flashy palette would.

Editorial Sections

I gave more spacing between sections compared to a typical e-commerce layout. Each product gets its own space instead of being packed into a tight grid.

Minimal Copy

I kept the headlines short. The design relies on the images and whitespace instead of long text descriptions.

Outcome

Personal project. Designed and prototyped in Figma as a self directed take on quiet luxury fashion branding and e-commerce UX.
