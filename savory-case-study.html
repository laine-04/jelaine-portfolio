Overview

The Brief

Savory Bites is a personal email design project for a fictional food brand. Unlike my other projects, this one was deliberately design and creativity forward: bold colors, expressive layouts, and a strong visual identity that makes the food the hero and every scroll feel like a craving.

The goal was to move away from minimal and explore what a promotional, high energy email design could look like when creativity leads.

Process

How I Approached It

01

Color and Font Planning

I started by defining the color story: bold reds, warm yellows, and deep browns that feel appetizing and energetic. I picked typography that feels bold and confident, matching the brand's personality.

02

Fluid, Continuous Layout

Instead of designing section by section with hard breaks, I used a more continuous, flowing layout where sections bleed into each other. This creates a more immersive scrolling experience, so the design feels like one cohesive piece instead of a stack of blocks.

03

Design in Figma

With the direction set, I built the full email in Figma, layering food photography, bold type, curved shapes, and color blocks to create a layout that feels dynamic and appetite driven.

Key Decisions

Design Choices

Bold Color First

I chose the palette before anything else. Rich reds and warm yellows immediately signal warmth, flavor, and energy, setting the tone for everything that follows.

Continuous Flow Design

Instead of traditional section blocks, I used curved shapes and overlapping elements to create a seamless, flowing experience that keeps the reader engaged as they scroll.

Food as the Hero

I used large, close up food photography throughout. The design is built around making the products look as good as possible, so the layout serves the food instead of the other way around.

Creative but Structured

Despite the bold, expressive direction, the layout still has clear hierarchy and readable sections. The goal was to be eye catching without being chaotic.

Outcome

Personal project. A creative, design forward email concept exploring bold visual storytelling and continuous layout design in a promotional food brand context.
