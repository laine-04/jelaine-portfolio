Overview

The Brief

Verdant & Co is a self initiated email newsletter design for a fictional plant lifestyle brand. The goal was to create an email that introduces the brand, shows off their plants, and gives genuinely useful educational content, all in one well structured layout.

The challenge was balancing brand personality with content clarity, especially for the educational sections where accuracy and readability mattered.

Process

How I Approached It

01

Content Planning

Before designing, I mapped out what the email should contain: brand intro, plant of the month, care tips, and a styling section. Getting the content structure right first made the layout decisions a lot easier later on.

02

Research for Accuracy

For the educational parts like the plant care tips and care guides, I researched to make sure the information was accurate and actually helpful, not just filler content.

03

Design in Figma

With the content and structure planned, I moved into Figma to design the full email layout. I used plant illustrations and photography while keeping the layout clean and easy to scan.

Key Decisions

Design Choices

Plant Accents, Not Clutter

I used botanical illustrations and plant imagery throughout to add personality and brand feel, without overwhelming the layout or making it hard to read.

Clean, Organized Sections

Each content block has clear visual separation. Readers can scan and jump to what interests them instead of wading through a wall of content.

Educational Content First

I put genuinely useful content front and center. This builds trust with the reader instead of leading purely with promotion.

Warm, Natural Palette

I used greens, creams, and earthy tones to reflect the brand's connection to nature and mindful living, without feeling sterile or corporate.

Outcome

Personal project. A self initiated email newsletter design exploring how to balance brand personality with educational content in a clean, organized layout.
